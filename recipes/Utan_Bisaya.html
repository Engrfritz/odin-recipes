Utan Bisaya (Law-Uy)

Favorite protein powerhouse of the Cebuanos

Utan Bisaya or Law-Uy stands in contrast to the heavy, hearty ,meat meals we're more familiar with. This humble dish boasts an array of vegetables you could easily grow in your backyard. By just boiling these veggies in water, this soup brings out the natural flavors of these vegetables - making it all around a perfectly healthy broth.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 lb squash
  • 2 pieces taro
  • 1 piece Knorr Shrimp Cube
  • 4 thumbs ginger
  • 1 piece Chinese eggplant, sliced
  • 10 pieces string beans, cut in 2 inch pieces
  • 12 pieces okra
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 3 1/2 cups water
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a cooking pot. Add lemongrass and ginger. Cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add taro and squash. Cover and continue boiling for 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Remove lemongrass from the pot. Add okra, string beans, and eggplant. Stir. Cover and continue cooking for 7 minutes.
  4. Add Knorr Shrimp Cube. Stir and cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Add spinach. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and ground black pepper.
  7. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve.
  8. Share and enjoy!
