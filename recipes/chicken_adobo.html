Chicken Adobo

The most loved Chicken Recipe in the Philippines.

This recipe is made up of chicken slices cooked in soy sauce and vinegar with garlic. This is a Filipino chicken dish that you can eat for lunch with warm white rice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 3 pcs dried bay leaves
  • 8 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

Instructions:

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat a cooking pot and pour the cooking oil.
  3. When the oil is hot enough, pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side.
  4. Pour-in the remaining marinade, including garlic. Add water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add dried bay leaves and whole peppercorn. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken gets tender.
  6. Add vinegar. Stir and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Put-in the sugar, and salt. Stir and turn the heat off. Serve hot.
